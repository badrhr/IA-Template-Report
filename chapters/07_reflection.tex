\noindent\textbf{Challenges Encountered.}
\textit{[REPLACE: Describe the most significant challenges you faced during

% --- Paragraph 4: What you would do differently ---
\noindent\textbf{What I Would Do Differently.}
\textit{[REPLACE: If you were to start this project again with the knowledge

your original plan and whether they were justified.]}

% --- Paragraph 5: Impact on future work and development ---
\noindent\textbf{Impact on Future Development.}
\textit{[REPLACE: Reflect on how this project experience has shaped your 
thinking about future work. What new questions has it raised for you? How has 
it influenced your understanding of the field or your own capabilities as a 
researcher or developer?]}
