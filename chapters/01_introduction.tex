\subsection{Background}
\label{sec:into_back}
Describe to a reader the context of your project. That is, what is your project 
and what is its motivation. Briefly explain the major theories, applications, 
and/or products/systems/algorithms whichever is relevant to your project.

\textbf{Caution:} Do not say you chose this project because of your interest, 
or your supervisor proposed it, or you were assigned it as your final year project. 
This may all be true, but it is not meant to be written here.

\subsection{Aims and Objectives}
\label{sec:intro_aims_obj}
Describe the ``aims and objectives'' of your project.

\textbf{Aims:} The aims tell a reader what you want/hope to achieve at the end 
of the project. The aims define your intent and purpose in general terms.

\textbf{Objectives:} The objectives are a set of tasks you would perform in order 
to achieve the defined aims. The objective statements must be specific and 
measurable through the results and outcomes of the project.
